# Agility and Number of Attacks

*Source: [TELE-ARENA SHRINE](https://tele-arena.tumblr.com/agility)*

## Overview

Agility significantly impacts gameplay mechanics in Tele-Arena. The statistic
influences movement speed, melee accuracy, and — most importantly — determines
how many attacks a character can execute per combat round.

## How Your Agility Determines Your Number of Attacks

The level shown is the character level at which each successive attack becomes
available, given the agility range.

| Class | Agility Range | 1st Attack | 2nd Attack | 3rd Attack | 4th Attack | 5th Attack | 6th Attack |
|-------|---------------|-----------|-----------|-----------|-----------|-----------|-----------|
| All Four Melee Classes | 5-14 | Lvl 1 | Lvl 3 | Lvl 6 | Lvl 9 | Lvl 12 | — |
| All Four Melee Classes | 15-29 | — | Lvl 1 | Lvl 3 | Lvl 6 | Lvl 9 | — |
| All Four Melee Classes | 30 | — | — | Lvl 1 | Lvl 4 | SKIP | Lvl 8 |
| Acolyte | 5-14 | Lvl 1 | Lvl 4 | Lvl 8 | Lvl 12 | Lvl 16 | — |
| Acolyte | 15-29 | — | Lvl 1 | Lvl 4 | Lvl 8 | Lvl 12 | — |
| Druid & Necrolyte | 5-14 | Lvl 1 | Lvl 5 | Lvl 10 | Lvl 15 | Lvl 20 | — |
| Druid & Necrolyte | 15-29 | — | Lvl 1 | Lvl 5 | Lvl 10 | Lvl 15 | — |
| Sorceror | 5-14 | Lvl 1 | Lvl 6 | Lvl 12 | Lvl 18 | Lvl 24 | — |
| Sorceror | 15-29 | — | Lvl 1 | Lvl 6 | Lvl 12 | Lvl 18 | — |

Melee classes are Warrior, Hunter, Archer, and Rogue. Spellcasting classes
(Acolyte, Druid, Necrolyte, Sorceror) follow similar progressions but with
different level requirements for each attack tier.

## Key Recommendations

Players should prioritize high starting agility during character creation:

- Goblin Rogues should target 30 agility for maximum attack frequency
- Goblin Warriors/Hunters/Archers benefit from 27+ agility to reach 6 attacks
  post-promotion
- Half-ogre spellcasters should aim for 15 agility to trigger attack scaling
- Most other builds work best with 15+ starting agility

## Stat Growth Mechanics

Statistics can increase randomly during leveling, capping at 25 unless the
character promotes. Players may also donate 100 gold at the Temple for random
stat boosts, though this method only affects stats below 20.
